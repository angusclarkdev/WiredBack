About

WiredBack is a re-insurance intermediary which develops risk mitigation strategies and re-insurance products that provide the broadest and most effective coverage. Our aim is to create value and worth for businesses and communities to thrive and prosper. Financial and professional lines is our principal area of expertise. We live in a world which is becoming exponentially more complicated and unpredictable. We are experiencing significant geo-political and socio-economic change amidst a technological revolution that is forcing us all to re-examine our exposure to risk. Individuals, businesses and institutions across the globe are having to design and create new strategies - new ways of doing - if they are to adapt to the new world order of big data and digital connectivity.

New Horizons

Uncertainty creates opportunity. Fortunately, G20 countries are enjoying the fastest rate of growth in world trade since 2011. While still short of pre-credit crunch levels, this shows real potential for mid-term to longer-term growth. This is increasing demand for re-insurance products, particularly in re-emerging markets and territories that require a range of specialist insurance solutions for large, state led infrastructure investment programmes.

We source Alternative Risk Transfer (ART) products from A or AA rated insurers which can provide cross border sovereign and sub-sovereign non-repayment default obligation. Products which underpin and guarantee any definable asset receivable that is date certain of creating future value! Transaction Mercantile

TML is a funding structure which is capable of financing virtually any project where there are clearly identified future cash flows. This financing approach takes a future contract obligation to pay and:

  • Establishes an underlying commercial structure between Buyer and Supplier
  • Breaks structure down into invoice(s) that fix the currency amounts and future dates when this will be paid, date certain
  • Utlises proof event blockchain technology
